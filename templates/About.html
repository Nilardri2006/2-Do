About This To-Do App

This simple yet efficient To-Do app is built with Flask, SQLAlchemy, and Jinja2. It helps users manage their daily tasks with ease — from creating to deleting tasks — all in a clean and interactive UI.

Designed with simplicity in mind, it's perfect for students, professionals, and anyone who loves to stay organized and productive.
